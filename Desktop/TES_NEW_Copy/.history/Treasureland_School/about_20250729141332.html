Welcome to Treasureland Elementary School

Welcome to Treasureland Elementary School, a premier educational institution in Ijebu Ode, Nigeria, where we nurture young minds to become future leaders. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been committed to providing quality education that combines academic excellence with strong moral values. Our child-centered approach ensures that each student's unique needs and potential are at the forefront of our educational practices.

Our Philosophy

We believe in holistic education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit of every child.

Our Approach

Interactive learning methods that encourage curiosity and critical thinking.

At Treasureland, we believe in holistic development, offering a balanced curriculum that includes academics, sports, arts, and character education. Our experienced and dedicated teachers use innovative teaching methods to make learning engaging and meaningful.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Treasureland Elementary School began with a vision to provide quality education that combines global standards with local values. Over the years, we have grown into a leading educational institution known for academic excellence and character development.

Safety First

At Treasureland Elementary, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our students. Our school environment is designed to be secure, nurturing, and supportive, ensuring that every child feels safe and valued.

Regular Classes

At Treasureland Elementary, we offer a wide range of classes to meet the diverse needs of our students. Our curriculum is designed to be engaging, relevant, and challenging, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Certified Teachers

At Treasureland Elementary, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible education. Our teachers are highly qualified and experienced, with a deep understanding of the subjects they teach. They are dedicated to helping students develop a love of learning and a strong foundation for future success.

Sufficient Classrooms

At Treasureland Elementary, we understand the importance of a safe and comfortable learning environment. Our classrooms are designed to be spacious, well-lit, and equipped with modern facilities to support the needs of our students.

Creative Lessons

At Treasureland Elementary, we believe that learning should be engaging and fun. Our lessons are designed to be interactive and hands-on, allowing students to explore and discover new ideas in a safe and supportive environment.

Sports Facilities

At Treasureland Elementary, we provide a range of sports facilities to support our students' physical and mental well-being. Our facilities include a well-equipped gymnasium, a playground, and a variety of sports equipment to encourage active participation in physical activities.

13 Years of Excellence

For over a decade, Treasureland Elementary School has been shaping young minds and building futures. Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education has made us a beacon of learning in our community. Join us as we continue our journey of nurturing tomorrow's leaders.
