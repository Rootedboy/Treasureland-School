Our Academic Structure

At Treasureland Elementary School, we offer a comprehensive educational journey from early childhood through primary education, designed to nurture every child's potential.

Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)

Nurturing the youngest minds with love, care, and early learning experiences that lay the foundation for lifelong success.

Our Approach

Our EYFS program follows the British Early Years Foundation Stage framework, adapted to our Nigerian context. We focus on seven key areas of learning and development:

  • Communication and Language
  • Physical Development
  • Personal, Social and Emotional Development
  • Literacy
  • Mathematics
  • Understanding the World
  • Expressive Arts and Design

Program Highlights

Age Groups

We accept children from 3 months to 2 years in our creche and 2-5 years in our preschool program.

Qualified Caregivers

Our staff are trained in early childhood education and first aid, ensuring your child receives the best care.

Holistic Development

We focus on cognitive, emotional, social, and physical development through play-based learning.

Play-Based Learning

Our creche program emphasizes learning through play, helping children develop social, emotional, and cognitive skills in a safe and nurturing environment.

Early Development

We focus on the holistic development of each child, including language acquisition, motor skills, and social interaction through age-appropriate activities.

Safe Environment

Our dedicated staff ensures a secure and hygienic environment where your little ones can explore, learn, and grow with confidence.

Preschool Department

A nurturing environment where our youngest learners explore, discover, and develop foundational skills through play-based learning.

Program Highlights

Our Montessori Approach

Nurturing independent, confident learners through the Montessori method

The Five Montessori Curriculum Areas

We implement the five (5) Montessori curriculum areas to provide a comprehensive educational experience:

  • Practical Life Exercise - Developing independence and life skills
  • Sensorial Education - Refining the senses and cognitive development
  • Mathematics - Concrete to abstract number concepts
  • Language - Building strong communication skills
  • Cultural Subjects - Exploring science, geography, history, and arts

Enrichment Programs

Our comprehensive program includes various co-curricular activities that complement the Montessori curriculum:

  • Music and Movement
  • Physical Education
  • Language Studies
  • Dance
  • Literary Arts

Our Educational Outcomes

At Treasureland School, each child is given the opportunity to develop:

  • Independence and self-reliance
  • Strong social skills
  • Healthy self-esteem
  • Fine and gross motor coordination
  • Self-discipline and order
  • Creativity and problem-solving

Our proven approach ensures that children typically learn to read and write without stress between the ages of 4 and 5 years, while developing strong number concepts. We are committed to nurturing cultured, enthusiastic, confident, and independent young individuals who are well-prepared for future academic challenges.
