Our History

Treasureland Elementary School (TES) as a Montessori school was established on the 19th September 2011 under the proprietorship of Mrs. Ebun Elizabeth Ogunyemi and Mr. Rapheal Adebola Ogunyemi. The couple had secured a two and half acres of land at Government Reservation Area (GRA), Ijebu Ode which serves as the permanent site of the school.

When the school started it had seven pupils on roll. Treasureland has grown tremendously in size and standard with a growing population of over 300 pupils and an excellent workforce. Treasureland Elementary School has been family-supporting from the start. The management is effectively guided by a formidable Board of Governors.
