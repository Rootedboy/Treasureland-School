Founder's Message

Home About Founder's Message

Mr.R.A Ogunyemi

Founder & Director

Dear Parents,

Welcome to Treasureland Elementary School, where we have been dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love of learning for the past 13 years. As the founder of this esteemed institution, I am thrilled to have the opportunity to share a few words with you.

Over the course of my 30-year career in education, I have seen how transformative a solid educational foundation can be for a child's future. This conviction led to the creation of this citadel of learning, and from the very beginning, our mission has been to cultivate an environment where every child feels valued, supported, and inspired to achieve their highest potential.

At Treasureland Elementary School, we are committed to providing a holistic education that goes beyond academic excellence. We strive to develop well-rounded individuals who are not only knowledgeable but also compassionate, creative, innovative, and resilient. Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to ensure that each pupil receives personalized attention and opportunities to explore their interests and talents.

As we look back on the past 13 years, we are proud of the achievements and progress we have made. However, our journey is far from over. We are continually evolving and adapting to meet the changing needs of our pupils and the world around us. We are excited about the future and the endless possibilities it holds for us.

Thank you for entrusting us with your child's education. We are honored to be a part of their journey and look forward to many more years of growth, learning, and success together.

Warm regards,
R.A Ogunyemi
