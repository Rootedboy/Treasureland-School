Our Extracurricular Activities

At Treasureland Elementary, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular programs are designed to nurture talents, develop new skills, and provide students with opportunities to explore their interests in a supportive and engaging environment.

Arts & Crafts

Our Arts & Crafts program encourages creative expression through various mediums. Students explore painting, sculpture, and mixed media while developing fine motor skills and artistic appreciation in a supportive, inspiring environment.

Music Club

In our Music Club, students discover the joy of music through singing, playing instruments, and music theory. They develop rhythm, coordination, and teamwork while exploring different musical genres and performance techniques.

Dance Group

Our Dance Group offers students the opportunity to explore various dance styles including contemporary, cultural, and modern dance. Through regular practice and performances, students build confidence, discipline, and teamwork while expressing themselves through movement.

Brigade

The Brigade program instills discipline, leadership, and teamwork through structured physical activities. Students participate in marching drills, team sports, and character-building exercises that promote physical fitness and personal responsibility.

Culinary Arts

Our culinary program introduces young chefs to the art of cooking, teaching essential kitchen skills, food safety, and nutrition while exploring diverse cuisines and developing a love for healthy eating.

Tailoring

Our Tailoring program teaches students the fundamentals of sewing, pattern making, and fabric selection. Participants develop practical skills in garment construction while fostering creativity and attention to detail in a hands-on learning environment.

Press Club

The Press Club nurtures young journalists and media enthusiasts. Students learn reporting, interviewing, and digital media skills while producing school publications and broadcasts, developing critical thinking and communication abilities in the process.

Beadmaking

In our Beadmaking workshop, students explore the colorful world of bead art. They learn various techniques for creating beautiful jewelry and decorative items while developing fine motor skills and an appreciation for traditional craftsmanship.

Vocational Club

Our Vocational Club introduces students to practical life skills and career exploration. Through hands-on activities and industry visits, participants gain exposure to various trades and professions, helping them discover their interests and potential career paths.

JET Program

JET (Junior Entrepreneurs Training) equips students with practical business skills and financial literacy. Through interactive projects and real-world challenges, young entrepreneurs develop leadership qualities, innovative thinking, and problem-solving abilities for future success.

Hair & Beauty

Our Hair & Beauty workshop teaches students essential grooming and personal care skills. Through hands-on activities, participants learn about hair care, basic styling techniques, and personal presentation while building confidence and self-expression.
