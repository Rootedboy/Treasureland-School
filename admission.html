Join Our Community

We're excited that you're considering Treasureland Elementary School for your child's education. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

Admission Process

1

Inquiry & School Tour

Schedule a visit to our campus to learn more about our programs and see our facilities. You can call us or fill out the inquiry form below.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit the required documents. Application forms are also available at the school office.

3

Assessment

Your child will be scheduled for an age-appropriate assessment to determine their readiness for the program.

4

Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision within one week of the assessment. Successful candidates will receive an admission letter.

5

Payment & Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process by paying the required fees and submitting all necessary documents.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or international passport
  • 4 recent passport photographs
  • Medical report and immunization records
  • Previous school reports (for transfer students)
  • Transfer certificate (for transfer students)
  • Testimonial from previous school (if applicable)
